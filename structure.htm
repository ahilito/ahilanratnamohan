2027 - Invasive Language - zaalvoorstelling

Inspired by my gradual alienation from Australia, I attempt to document the deterioration of my English, while considering the parallels between the migration of words and plants.

2028 - De Stemtest - theatre performance    UNTRANSLATABLE


Ahilan attempts to get the Flemish National Radio's "Voice Accreditation."  Together with renowned Flemish radio journalist, Friedl Lesage.  He steps onto the stage to examine his Dutch, while putting a mirror on the systems and structures which maintain it as a language.


theatre | performance | staged research | language discrimination

2028 - FxF Suite [community project]

9 or more female and non-binary footballers on stage.  A football-dance-theatre performance that explores exclusion and inclusion.  We delve into the extreme collective experience of this non-male Brussels football community, and its activism and sense of belonging.  Through a collective dance, we explore the line between rage and joy.


football | dance | theatre | football x fashion offshoot

2029 - Raumdeuter [theatre performance]

A selection of 6 of Ahil's long-term collaborating football-performers - in a soft contact with a roaming audience - research space as a construct in football.  An attempt to create and organise space via the codes and rituals of football.  An attempt at translating the feeling of standing and moving body-to-body in a match to the theatre.


football| dance | theatre | abstract yet playful | choreographic research

ONGOING - Pidgin X [community, multiple malleable outcomes]


We talk about being homesick.  But is it possible to be languagesick?  Languagelonging, perhaps?


In Pidgin X we create a new language based (a) on the words which locals, who have had to learn the language, miss from their mother tongue and (b) their 'grammatic complaints'.  Working with a network of people who have had to learn the local language, we come together through interviews and 'language boards' to re-imagine the local language.


politics of language | realising fictions | participative
