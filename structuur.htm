ONGOING - Pidgin X [community, multiple malleable outcomes]


We talk about being homesick.  But is it possible to be languagesick?  Languagelonging, perhaps?


In Pidgin X we create a new language based (a) on the words which locals, who have had to learn the language, miss from their mother tongue and (b) their 'grammatic complaints'.  Working with a network of people who have had to learn the local language, we come together through interviews and 'language boards' to re-imagine the local language.


language | realising fictions | participative
